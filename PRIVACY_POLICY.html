Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how CarDealership ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and other authentication credentials when you create an account
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle details including brand, model, chassis number, condition, images, and related documents
  • Customer Information: Customer names, contact information, identification documents, and transaction history
  • Broker Information: Broker details, contact information, and commission-related data
  • Transaction Data: Purchase and sale records, payment information, EMI schedules, and financial transaction details
  • Documents and Images: Photos of vehicles, identification documents, and other business-related documents you upload

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Service, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (features accessed, time spent in the app, error logs)
  • Network information (IP address, network type)

1.3 Camera and Storage Permissions

Our app requires access to your device's camera and storage to:

  • Capture photos of vehicles and documents
  • Store and manage business-related images and files

We only access these features when you explicitly use them within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • To manage vehicle inventory and dealership operations
  • To process transactions and track payments
  • To manage customer and broker relationships
  • To generate catalogs and business reports
  • To authenticate users and secure your account
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Firestore and Firebase Storage), which provide industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

While we implement appropriate security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers (such as Firebase/Google) who assist us in operating our app and providing services
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database storage, and file storage. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information stored in the app
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
